Hi There, hope you are having a good time!!!...The Web app that you are seeing on your screen right now is about calssifying whether the selected image by the user is having a face wihtout a mask or with the mask.To perform classification first select a image which contains a face with or without mask. After Selecting the image press the button that says "Pass the image through neural network", this will pass the image through the neural network and the result will be obtained on the extreme right hand side of the screen, with a label stating "Face" or "Mask" depending on the Image Passed. :)

What is Actually Happening????

The selected image is passed through a resnet34 neural network, whihc is trained to classify between the masked and non-masked faces. The Model used is a pytorch model wiht pickle format.The model is loaded when the site is opened, once the button is clicked ".predict" method is runned on the model and the prediction is obtained.
